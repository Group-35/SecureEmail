Basic Concepts

 

a) Introduction:

A clean, intuitive, and secure messaging service to communicate with other users.

Keep all of your conversations completely private from hackers.

Create an account and begin securely messaging your friends today!

Go to https://salty-depths-8905.herokuapp.com/users/sign_in to start.

b) Requirements:

             1) You will need a desktop PC or laptop with a Windows, Linux, or Unix based operating system.

      2) You will need an internet connection.

      3) You will need a keyboard.

4) You will need a modern web browser

Login Page 

 

The login page is the gateway to Securemail. Users can login into a preexisting account or create a new account by following the link Sign up.

 

Account Creation

Users can create a new account by inputting their:

Desired Username

Email address

First Name

Last Name

Password with a minimum length of 8 characters

 

Upon submission, you will be redirected to your new Indox.

 Inbox

 

Your inbox is the core of your Securemail experience. All new secure messages will be displayed here in order of most recent.

Contacts List

The Contacts List is list of all contacts. New contacts can be added by username by clicking Add contact.

Spam

Users can block users from sending messages to themselves by adding the username to the blacklist.

Composing a new message

Users can create and encrypt new messages in the Compose Message tab.

The recipients username will need to be entered in the To field.

The desired message will need to be entered into the Message field.

 

To send the message, click encrypt. Your message will be encrypted for you to view.

After verifying that the message is encrypted, click send to send the message.

Changing Your Settings

Any changes to your account can be made in the settings tab.

To change your password, enter your new password in the Password and Password confirmation fields.

Enter your old password in the Current Password to authorize the change.

 

To delete your account, simply click Cancel my account.
