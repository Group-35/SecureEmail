Document Standard

Web artifacts:

Font: Normal, 10 pt, black

Headings: Section headings are bolded and black font. Titles of document pages must be 10 size font.

Spacing: A horizontal line separates each section of the document. Each section’s contents will be indented once from the section title.

Spell and Grammar checking: David Clapp and Thomas Tavarez will proofread each page for uniformity and correctness.

Table of Contents: Table of contents must be labeled, with the title centered in size 10 bold font.

Modification History table: This will include all modification history in size 10, black, Normal font. This will be placed at the top of each document. There will be three columns: Date modified, person who made modifications, and overview of modifications.
